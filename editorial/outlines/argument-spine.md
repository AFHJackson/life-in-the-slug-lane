## OVERVIEW: The Reader Journey

### Three Phases of Reader Development

| Phase | Chapters | Reader Outcome |
|-------|----------|----------------|
| **RECOGNITION** | Prologue, 1-2 | "I already live in multiple systems" |
| **UNDERSTANDING** | 3-6 | "I know how each habitat works" |
| **NAVIGATION** | 7-10, Epilogue | "I can operate wisely across all four" |

### Central Argument (One Sentence)

**The political systems we fight about (communism, socialism, capitalism, anarchy) aren't competing ideologies—they're complementary tools that work at different scales, and we already use all four every day without realizing it.**

### Book Promise to Reader

By the end of this book, you will:
1. Recognize you already navigate four systems daily
2. Understand why each system works at its proper scale
3. Navigate hybrids and choose the right tool for each situation
4. Steward your excess toward discovery and generosity
5. See what we can build together when we stop the wrong fight

- Must pre-fund collectively
- Scale: community to institution
