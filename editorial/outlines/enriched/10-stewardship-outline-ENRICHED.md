## LAYER 5: EXAMPLE SPECIFICATIONS

### Primary Story: Matt's Week (Ring Navigator Profile)

**Use full content from:** `new-content-ch10-matt-navigator.md`

**Mandatory Scene Elements:**

**Monday: The Family Decision (Market habitat)**
- Hendersons in showroom with three kids
- Matt's backstory: 20 years ago would have upsold, missed daughter's recital
- "The best boat isn't the one with the most features. It's the one you actually use."
- Sells mid-tier, $4,000 less commission—but they'll be back

**Tuesday: The Commons Problem (Safety Commons)**
- Planning board, developer wants to rezone ag land
- Matt: "Net job creation? Maybe twelve. And we lose community anchors."
- "Takes one to know one"—he can smell a bad contract
- Home at 9:15, Carol saved dinner

**Wednesday: The Scholarship Sale (Friendship/Commons hybrid)**
- Rotary at 7 AM, Matt: "Trade schools."
- Tyler's photo—wants marine mechanics, didn't know scholarship existed
- "Same way I sell boats—helping people see what they actually want"
- Club votes unanimously

**Friday: The Volunteer Event (Friendship)**
- Pickleball tournament for Habitat for Humanity
- Donated equipment, got sponsors, declines speaking role
- "I'm just here to help. You say the words."
- Voluntary, emergent, he just shows up and is useful

**Sunday: The Integration (Family)**
- Back porch with coffee, grandkids with slip-n-slide
- Email from Tyler: "Got accepted. Scholarship came through."
- Different tools for different scales
- "This was stewardship. Not control. Not optimization. Just wisdom."

**The Key Passages:**
> "Different tools for different scales. Different habitats for different relationships. He didn't try to run his family like a business anymore."

> "It had taken him twenty years to learn this. Cost him relationships with his kids when they were young—relationships he'd spent the last twenty years rebuilding."

> "This was what it looked like when you learned to navigate The Ring."

### Stewardship Definition Table

**What Stewardship IS:**

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Operating inside systems you didn't create | You inherited The Ring |
| Honoring each habitat at its scale | Right tool for right situation |
| Moving through with wisdom, not control | Humility + agency |

**What Stewardship is NOT:**

| NOT | Why |
|-----|-----|
| Passivity | You have agency, responsibility, obligation |
| Ownership | You don't control the systems |
| Ideology | Navigation, not prescription |

| Restraint | Doesn't force one tool everywhere, knows when NOT to act |
| Generosity | Deploys excess consistently, discovery and generosity both |
| Long-term orientation | Plays infinite game, builds for next generation |
