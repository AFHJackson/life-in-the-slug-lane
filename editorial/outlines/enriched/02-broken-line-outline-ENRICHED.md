## LAYER 5: EXAMPLE SPECIFICATIONS

### Primary Story: Evacuation Zone Argument (Sarah and Lauren)

**Mandatory Scene Elements:**

**Setting:**
- 6 AM morning walk
- Suburban neighborhood
- Three years of walking together
- These are friends, not enemies

**The Trigger:**
- News story about man arrested entering evacuation zone
- He was trying to rescue a friend
- Arrests triggered by system enforcement

**The Argument:**

**Sarah's Position (must convey):**
- "He's a grown man, he has the right—"
- Individual competence matters
- You don't infantilize adults
- Scale: individual autonomy

**Lauren's Position (must convey):**
- "But the system needs to—"
- Coordination requires rules
- Rescue teams can't do their job with civilians wandering
- Scale: population safety

**The Stuck:**
- Neither convinces the other
- Neither is sure they're right
- Both feel frustrated
- "They'd been walking together for three years. This was the first time they couldn't find each other."

**Emotional Beat:**
- Reader should feel: "I've been in this argument"
- Reader should wonder: "Why does this happen?"
- Answer is the map, not the people

**Do NOT:**
- Declare either one right (both are, at different scales)
- Moralize about the policy
- Resolve the argument (it's stuck because of the map)

### Historical Framework: The Left/Right Line Origins

**French Revolution (must include):**
- 1789: Estates-General in Versailles
- King supporters → right side
- Revolution supporters → left side
- "A seating chart became a spectrum"
- Key phrase: "furniture arrangement"

**Industrial Revolution (must include):**
- Class conflict maps onto line
- Left = workers, collective
- Right = owners, property
- Line hardens into economic identity

**Cold War (must include):**
- Binary superpower standoff
- Capitalism vs. Communism
- No room for nuance
- "Survival felt like it depended on picking a side"

**Modern Media (must include):**
- 24-hour news needs winners/losers
- Team loyalty > policy analysis
- Line becomes entertainment
- Scoreboard mentality
