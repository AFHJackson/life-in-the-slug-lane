## LAYER 5: EXAMPLE SPECIFICATIONS

### Primary Story: The 3:17 AM Fire Response

**Mandatory Scene Elements:**

**Setup:**
- 3:17 AM
- Smoke alarm screaming
- Kids crying
- "This is not a dream. This is not a drill."

**Key Moments (all must appear):**

1. **The Crisis:**
   - Grab the kids
   - Get out
   - Standing on lawn in the dark
   - "Smoke is now pouring from the second-floor window"

2. **The Call:**
   - 911
   - "What's your address?"
   - "Fire trucks are on their way"
   - Waiting in the dark

3. **The Response:**
   - "In less than ten minutes, you hear the sirens"
   - Firefighters arrive—strangers who came before you asked
   - "They don't know you, don't ask your income, don't check your voting record"
   - "We will come, whoever you are"

4. **The Realization:**
   - "Nobody negotiates a price per gallon of water"
   - Pre-funded by people who never met you
   - Truck purchased in a budget meeting you didn't attend
   - Training happened before you needed it

**Emotional Beat:**
- Fear → relief → gratitude → recognition
- "This is socialism. Real socialism. The kind that actually works."

### The Four Rules (each needs specific verification):

| Rule | What It Means | Verification Question |
|------|---------------|----------------------|
| Risk Pooling | Sized for worst days | "Fire trucks sit idle. Waste or readiness?" |
| Time Pressure | Emergencies collapse choice | "Did you shop in your last emergency?" |
| Impartiality | "We will come, whoever you are" | "Expect them to ask your income?" |
| Standardization | "Same CPR in every living room" | "Want innovative CPR on you?" |

### Satirical Example: Uber for House Fires

**Must Include:**
> "Imagine your house is on fire and your phone buzzes: 'We found a driver! Jose is 8 minutes away in a 2014 ladder truck. He has a 4.7 rating and surge pricing is currently 3.4x due to high demand in your area.'"

**Must Convey:**
- The absurdity is visceral
- "Emergencies are bad for shopping"
- Not anti-market (markets are great elsewhere)
- This specific context breaks market logic
