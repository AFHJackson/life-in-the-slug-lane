## LAYER 5: EXAMPLE SPECIFICATIONS

### Primary Story: The Moving Day Scene

**Mandatory Scene Elements:**

**The Text:**
- Tuesday night, late
- "My lease is up Saturday and the truck just fell through"
- "Is there any way you could help me move?"

**The Response:**
- Within minutes the chat lights up
- "I've got a truck if we can do morning"
- "I can't lift much but I can watch kids"
- "I've got a dolly and some straps"
- "I can do a lunch run, what kind of pizza?"

**The Day:**
- Loose convoy forms
- "One friend with a truck, another with a hatchback full of boxes"
- Someone takes lead on logistics
- Someone becomes safety officer
- Someone is comic relief

**The Outcome:**
- "It is not efficient in any industrial sense"
- People show up late, someone forgot the tape
- "But by midafternoon, the impossible thing... has moved"
- "No one sends an invoice"
- "The only 'payment' is greasy pizza, sore backs, and the quietly satisfying sense that you belong"

**Key Quote:**
> "Nobody was really in charge, but it still worked."

**Emotional Beat:**
- Warmth, humor, recognition
- "That's my friend group"
- Anarchy revealed as ordinary

**Must Convey:**
